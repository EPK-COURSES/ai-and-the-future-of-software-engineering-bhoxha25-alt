**Topic:** AI Coding and Development Tools 

---

During this research, I learned that Artificial Intelligence is becoming an important part of software engineering. Before researching this topic, I mostly thought AI tools were only simple assistants for writing code faster.After reading articles and industry discussions, I understood that AI affects many parts of software development including testing, documentation, deployment, and project management.

The most surprising thing for me was how quickly companies are adopting AI tools such as GitHub Copilot and ChatGPT.Many developers already use these tools daily to improve productivity.

### 🔍 Areas of Concern
At the same time, I also discovered several concerns. One major concern is that developers may become too dependent on AI-generated code without fully understanding it. This could create security vulnerabilities and poor software quality. I think engineers must continue learning programming fundamentals instead of relying completely on automation.

### 🚀 Future Opportunities
I believe AI creates many opportunities for future software engineers. Developers can save time on repetitive tasks and focus more on creative problem solving, architecture design, and innovation. AI may also help beginners learn faster.

In my opinion, human skills will remain extremely important in the future. Communication, teamwork, critical thinking, and decision-making cannot easily be replaced by AI systems. Software engineering is not only about writing code; it also involves understanding people, solving business problems, and collaborating in teams.

### Conclusion
This research changed my opinion about the future of programming jobs. I no longer think AI will simply replace programmers.Instead, I think the role of software engineers will evolve. Developers who learn how to work together with AI tools will have better opportunities. Personally, I plan to adapt by continuously learning new technologies while also improving my problem-solving and communication skills. I believe future engineers must balance technical knowledge with adaptability and critical thinking.
